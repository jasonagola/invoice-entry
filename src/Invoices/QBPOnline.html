Ebike Battery Recycling


Now you can safely and reliably recycle damaged and end-of-life ebike batteries. This new service is provided by PeopleforBikes and Call2Recycle - the nation's largest, most reliable battery recycling program experts. At this time, this program is only available to US retailers in the contiguous 48 states.


Get Started

Ready to increase customer loyalty, bring in new ebike customers, and help the environment? Enroll your shop as a collection site and begin accepting batteries by clicking the link below. You'll complete a 20-minute training and take a short quiz. Then, you'll be sent collection kits, promotional materials, and be listed on the collection site locator online. Enrollment for retailers is free. Learn More.

Environmental Recycling Fee

Starting in summer 2022, US retailers in the contiguous 48 states will see a $15 environmental recycling fee (ERF) on the purchase of any ebike and ebike battery through QBP to fund this service.
